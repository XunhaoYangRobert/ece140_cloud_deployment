Laws of Design examples

Law of Pragnaz:

The icon under microphone and speaker plugin place is intuitive for human user to think of a mic and a head phone so that they can classify this clearly and plug in the right place.

Millers Law:

The wiki home page contains to much choices and selection on the left side of the website that people might be unable to reachout to the button they want.

Jacob's law:

The design of Find My App in iPhone has a navigation bar that is similar to what android device used to have. This kind of bottom navigation bar has become a common design choice for both the designers and users.
